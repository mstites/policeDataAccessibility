Data Quality

In addition to policing data being hard to acquire, there are also potential concerns when it comes to the quality of the data. For example, while I was able to find a number of openly available police datasets when searching national government databases, the usefulness of this data is questionable. Many of the datasets contained old or limited timelines of data, despite citing that they were supposed to be updated to the publicly accessible version as often as weekly (CITE 2-3 SPECIFIC DATASETS).
