Police Source Data This page is about answering what I think is the most important question of this project: can data projects get the police source data they need? When I say police source data, I am referring to the raw data that is collected by police departments that ideally, people can access to perform various analyses on. Examples of data projects using various forms of policing data can be seen on the Data Project Spotlight page.

Below I outline my process and an overview of the results of my searching experience. But you can also skip straight to the acquiring data section if you would just like to read the raw information on what it is like to obtain this data.
