Data Project Spotlight

Examples of a few different data projects. Selected some different types to share to try to reflect my general findings in my search.

Many of these projects were framed as "police accountability" in some way shape or form. Some very directly. At first, I was very thrown off by this, it seems very reformist. But for some of the projects, it does seem like it could be a method of getting the data, as they brand themselves in a way to say "hey police departments work with me". But at the end of the day, when you look at the reports from these data projects, they at least provide some quantification of the racism and violence that police departments are doing in their communities. They still tell a story, and that can have value. However, there certainly is also a sort of gaslightly (irony?) approach of pretending that there is an accountability possibility while also reporting that police are racial profiling at extreme levels and reporting high incidents of violence over sometimes long periods of time - this section would need to be re-written
